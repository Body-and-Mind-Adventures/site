About Us

MEET Simone
Simone is originally from the Netherlands, While studying to become a yoga teacher in India, something changed in her life causing her to want a new adventure. She loves the outdoors and wanted to combine it with her yoga skills. While studying to become a Yoga Teacher in India her live changed and wanted something new in her life. Simone want to share knowledge she gained to help others and to find their way back to optimal health and authentic happiness.
As an avid hiker and outdoors girl, She hiked many beautiful hikes in Hawaii, Oahu and developed a strong connection to nature that re-established a child-like wonder that can only be inspired by nature.

One Week Body, Mind And Adventure Retreats in India

For several years, mindfulness has been a buzzword for wellbeing. Its all about calming and focussing, focus the mind. Its worth to try. We will offer with this body and mind adventure a relaxed and frightening days or week/s for those who are completely new to this, and for those who have more experience. Retreats will take place several times a year in a wooded area near the eye in North Suffolk. Teach awareness techniques and gives participants plenty of time to try them out. Meals are vegetarian and accomodation is in wooden houses with bunk beds or in tents in the summer, although there is a Ram Jhula nearby for those who wants more comfort.
